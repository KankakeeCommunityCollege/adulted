KCC Foundation Committees

Investment/Finance

The Investment/Finance Committee is responsible for recommending and approving investment practices for Foundation assets. It provides counsel and advice concerning the endowment funds, currently gifted funds and unrestricted accounts.

Governance/Nominations

The Governance/Nominations Committee is responsible for ensuring compliance with the Foundation By-Laws as well as seeking and recommending candidates to fill board vacancies.
