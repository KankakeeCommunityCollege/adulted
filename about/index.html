The KCC Foundation helps advance the mission of KCC--enhancing quality of life through learning--by raising funds that support the college’s students, faculty, staff and programs.​​

Since 1969, the KCC Foundation has worked to secure funding for student scholarships, innovative programs, and enhancement of quality educational opportunities. Through the generous support of private donors and public agencies, the foundation contributes to the advancement of ​higher education.
