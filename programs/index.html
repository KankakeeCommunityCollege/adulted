GED

High School Equivalency Prep gets you ready to take and pass a high school equivalency test, including the GED. Sessions begin every eight weeks. There is no cost to the student, and books are included.

ESL

English as a Second Language (ESL) is a program to learn English or improve skills after growing up speaking a different language. It includes listening, speaking, reading and writing. There is no cost to the student.

Community Literacy Project

The Community Literacy Project offers basic reading, writing, computation and English courses. Instruction and materials are provided at no cost to the student.
