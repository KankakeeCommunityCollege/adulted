GED Prep

GED Prep prepares you to take and pass the GED® test. Sessions begin throughout the year. Classes are free and include resources at no cost to the student while enrolled.

ESL

English as a Second Language (ESL) classes teach English to non-native speakers. Instruction covers listening, speaking, reading and writing. Classes are free and include resources at no cost to the student.

Learn More

Community Literacy Project

The Literacy program provides volunteer tutors to help struggling readers with foundational skills in reading, writing and math. There is no cost to the student. Accepting volunteer applications.
