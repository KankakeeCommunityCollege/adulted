High School Equivalency (HSE)

High School Equivalency Prep gets you ready to take and pass a high school equivalency (HSE) test, including the GED and HiSET. Sessions begin throughout the year. Classes are free and books are provided during class time.

ESL

English as a Second Language (ESL) classes teach English to non-native speakers. It includes listening, speaking, reading and writing. Classes are free and books are provided during class time.

Community Literacy Project

The Literacy program provides volunteer tutors to help struggling readers with foundational skills in reading, writing and math. There is no cost to the student. Accepting volunteer applications.
