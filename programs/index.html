High School Equivalency (GED® / HiSet® )

High School Equivalency Prep gets you ready to take and pass a high school equivalency (HSE) test, including the GED and HiSET. Sessions begin throughout the year. Classes are free and books are provided during class time.

Learn More

New! GED+Career

GED+Career is a new program where qualified students can complete GED preparation and career training at the same time. Just like the general GED program, there is no cost to the student.

Learn More

ESL

English as a Second Language (ESL) classes teach English to non-native speakers. Instruction covers listening, speaking, reading and writing. Classes are free and books are provided during class time.

Learn More

Community Literacy Project

The Literacy program provides volunteer tutors to help struggling readers with foundational skills in reading, writing and math. There is no cost to the student. Accepting volunteer applications.
