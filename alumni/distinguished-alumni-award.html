John Fulton Distinguished Alumni Award

Established in 2009, the John M. Fulton Distinguished Alumni Award honors longtime KCC Alumni Association member John Fulton of Herscher. Fulton is a 1981 graduate of KCC and earned two associate degrees (business and general studies).

John Fulton is a local farmer, a friend, a decorated Vietnam War hero, and a tireless advocate for veterans affairs and for people with disabilities. He demonstrates the power of KCC alumni and is a source of pride and inspiration. In 2009, the KCC Foundation Board voted unanimously to name the top honor for KCC alumni "The John M. Fulton Distinguished Alumni Award."


Submit a nomination

Recipients of the award must meet the following criteria:

  • Outstanding success and distinction in chosen field
  • Humanitarian service that has proven greatly beneficial to society
  • Continued interest in/support of education and community colleges
  • Evidence of having overcome life's obstacles
  • Completion of a recognized program of instruction at Kankakee Community College
