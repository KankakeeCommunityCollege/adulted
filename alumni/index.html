The Power of Alumni Support

Every dollar counts! Your gift in any amount can assist current and future KCC students. Get creative and enjoy the Power of Giving!

  • Share your giving on social media and encourage fellow alumni to give to make a greater impact.
  • Give a gift in the amount of your class year.
  • Give an unrestricted gift to help where the need is greatest.
  • Share the reasons why you give with the KCC Foundation so that we can use your story to inspire others. Send us your story with your photo to alumni@kcc.edu
