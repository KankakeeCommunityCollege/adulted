Special Events

Scholarship Recognition Dinner

In many cases, scholarships can be a deciding factor in a student’s ability to attend college.

At the annual Scholarship Recognition Dinner, scholarship recipients and those who donated to their scholarship have the opportunity to meet. Recipients share their appreciation with donors and talk more about their plans for the future. Donors are able to see firsthand the impact their donations made and how their contributions transformed students’ lives.

The event is held in October. The 2017 dinner was Thursday, Oct. 19, at the Hilton Garden Inn, Kankakee. The following scholarships and awards were announced:

Century Scholars

KCC's 2017 Century Scholars are (left to right) Cassandra Hurst, Katelyn O'Dell and Taylor Bradley, shown here with KCC's president, Dr. John Avendano.

Each year since 1984, the Century Scholars Recognition Society has presented its scholarship award at the annual recognition dinner. The 2017 Century Scholars and Scholarship Recognition Dinner took place Thursday, Oct. ​19, 2017 at the Hilton Garden Inn, Kankakee.

2017 Distinguished Alumni

Each year, the KCC Foundation selects a KCC graduate to receive the John M. Fulton Distinguished Alumni Award. Congratulations to the 2017 Distinguished Alumni Award recipient, Donald Rehmer (A.S. 1994).
