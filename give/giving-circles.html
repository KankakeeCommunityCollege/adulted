Women’s Giving Circle: Educate, Empower and Endeavor

Grant assistance for second year students in need

KCC students who are in their second year can receive a grant when facing barriers to completion. This program requires a faculty referral and a faculty mentor to ensure that students stay on the path to cross the finish line of graduation. Assistance can include childcare, eldercare, food, healthcare, transportation and housing.

President's Circle

KCC’s President’s Circle recognizes donors who have contributed annual gifts of $1,000 or more. These gifts--in the form of annual donations, endowed scholarships, planned endowments and support for the college’s capital development--make a lasting impact for KCC students.

President’s Circle donors receive exclusive correspondence from KCC President Dr. John Avendano and are offered an inside information about impact our faculty, students and alumni are making on campus and in the community. Donors also receive recognition in publications and invitations to events.

Gifts from President’s Circle members have the power to transform the college experience for KCC students in a positive, lasting way. Gifts can be directed where they will make the most impact or can be tailored for specific interests.
