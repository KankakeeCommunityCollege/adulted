Creative Giving Ideas

If other giving options don’t fit your interests or your budget, consider these other ways to donate. Whatever you choose to donate will help the college transform students’ lives. Your gift, no matter what it is, will always count.

Help students learn

  • Donate to the Foundation's unrestricted fund; your donation will be used where the need is the greatest.
  • Purchase a portable station for the Nursing Simulation Lab.
  • Participate in the Scholarship Review Program. Requires a time commitment of 4-10 hours during March/April to review scholarship applications online.
  • Purchase One Button Studio - a video recording studio for KCC students to enhance and practice presentation, interviewing, language, and persuasive skills.
  • Donate your old car to the Automotive Technology program - department approval required.
  • Purchase new material for the KCC Library.
  • Sponsor a KCC event.
  • Shop through Amazon Smile to benefit the KCC Foundation.
  • Purchase a planishing hammer to be used in fabrication classes in the Manufacturing Technology program.

Help students in need

  • Donate to our Compassion Fund. Any amount will help.
  • Purchase a Campus Cafe gift card for a student who can't afford food.
  • Create your own scholarship fund.
​

Make an impact

  • Provide bench seating with a personal dedication on our scenic riverfront.
  • Sponsor athletics.
  • Donate toward beautification initiatives to create collaborative study spaces.
  • Name a classroom or building on campus.
